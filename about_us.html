When you entrust your most important data to a company, you need to know the company has the background and capacity to deliver. At COVA Systems we take that responsibility seriously, and we make sure the resources are always in place to protect your data.

Background

COVA Systems is a division of Leapfrog Technologies LLC, which was founded in 1995.  A sister company is BitStreet Internet, a regional Internet Service Provider.  We developed expertise in managing Points of Presence, provisioning Internet service, managing a regional network and network security. COVA was established in 2003 to leverage this experience and make the advantages of a world-class data facility affordable and available to a wide range of companies.


The Facility

Built by a Fortune 500 telecommunications company, the COVA data center occupies one of the upper floors of a premiere building in Abilene, Texas.  Aspects of connectivity and protection for housing corporate data and all Web hosting for a $20 billion corporation with millions of customers were designed into the center.  In 2003 COVA Systems completed significant upgrades to the center to make it a totally secure hosting and disaster recovery facility.
